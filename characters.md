**The Yeandel Sisters:**

- **Karisa Yeandel** — The youngest sister; returned to Beacon Hill after 5 years to reopen the family bookstore, now the target of a stalker.
- **Aesmine Yeandel** — The eldest sister; a successful international supermodel, cold and controlled but fiercely protective.
- **Millenka Yeandel** — The middle sister; the peacemaker who eloped with David seven years ago and became estranged from the family.

**Family & Past:**

- **Mr. Yeandel** — The sisters' late father; founded Yeandel Book Store in 1962, recently deceased.
- **David** — Millenka's husband; runs a construction company in Worcester.
- **Karia** — Karisa's former neighbor and friend; an investigative journalism student who died by falling from her balcony five years ago.

**Cathie's Group (Karisa's Friends):**

- **Cathie Fawckner** — Karisa's old friend who rallied help to restore the bookstore.
- **Margot** — Cathie's friend; tall and strong, took charge of cleaning the floors.
- **Sloane** — Cathie's friend (and apparent romantic interest); handles marketing/social media.
- **Priya & Jun** — A couple in Cathie's friend group who helped sort the bookshelves.
- **Vivienne** — Striking redhead in Cathie's group; knows plumbing, showed notable interest in Karisa.

**Antagonists:**

- **KARAM** — A mysterious stalker from five years ago who targeted young women in Beacon Hill; seemingly returned (or being impersonated).
- **The Man in the Black Hoodie** — KARAM's operative/watcher; surveils the sisters and delivers the threats.
- **Unknown Handler** — The person giving the hooded man instructions via text, orchestrating the "trap."

**Minor Characters:**

- **Mrs. Whitmore** — A regular bookstore customer who buys poetry.
- **Diane** — Aesmine's assistant.
- **Mrs. Patterson** — An old neighbor who kept in touch with Millenka.
